About Us

Welcome to Nova Educational Consultancy, a leading educational consultancy dedicated to empowering students in Ethiopia with the knowledge, skills, and guidance needed to achieve their academic and career aspirations. At Nova Educational Consultancy, we believe that every student is unique and has the potential to excel in their chosen field of study. Our mission is to provide personalized and comprehensive educational solutions to nurture and unlock this potential.


Our core values:

  • Student-Centric Approach: We place students at the heart of everything we do, ensuring that their individual needs and goals are met.
  • Expert Guidance: Our team of experienced education consultants and advisors are well-versed in the Ethiopian education system and can provide valuable insights into the global academic landscape.
  • Ongoing Support: We're committed to providing continuous support throughout the educational journey, from initial planning to post-graduation career advice.
